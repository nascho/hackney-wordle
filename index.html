Hackney - Wordle Application

How to Play:

  • - Each guess must be a valid five letter word.
  • - The colour of a tile will change to show you how close your guess was.
  • - If the tile turns GREEN, the letter is in the word and correct place.
  • - If the tile turns ORANGE, the letter is in the word but, not in the correct place.
  • - If the tile turns RED, the letter is not in the word.
  • - Use the keyboard to enter your letter from letter A-Z and use Delete to cancel a choice.
  • - Press Enter to submit your suggestion, per row.
